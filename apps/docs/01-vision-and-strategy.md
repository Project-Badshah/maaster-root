# 01. Vision & Strategy

## The Vision
The vision for the **Event Maaster Ecosystem (Project Badshah)** is to build highly scalable, enterprise-grade software targeting various aspects of the event and business management industry.

## Ecosystem Expansion
We operate on a "House of Apps" philosophy. We build distinct, highly-focused micro-SaaS applications.
1. **Photo Maaster**: A robust, scalable portal for managing event photography deliverables, client galleries, and asset distribution.
2. **Hisab Maaster**: A highly secure financial accounting and tracking software for event-related expenditures and ledgers.
3. **Dukan Maaster**: A robust e-commerce and storefront engine.

Once the current portfolio is fully operational at an enterprise level, we will begin exploring and integrating new business ventures into the Project Badshah portfolio. All future ventures must inherit the architectural and aesthetic DNA outlined in our Global Tech Laws.
